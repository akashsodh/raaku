🏏 PLAYING RULES

  • Har match mein overs admin set karega (5, 6, 8, 10, 15 ya 20 overs).
  • Har team ko minimum 11 players chahiye. Agar 11 nahi hain toh available players se khelo.
  • Ek bowler maximum match overs ke 20% bowl kar sakta hai (e.g. 10 overs match mein max 2 overs).
  • Ek bowler consecutive overs bowl nahi kar sakta — end change zaroori hai.
  • Wide ball: +1 run, extra delivery. Batsman wicket out nahi hoga (except run out).
  • No Ball: +1 run, free hit milegi agla ball par.
  • Leg Bye / Bye: Runs milenge lekin batsman ke stats mein count nahi honge.
  • Rain interruption par D/L method ya admin ka decision final hoga.

🔴 BATTING RULES

  • Batsman ko 1 minute mein pitch par aana hoga wicket girne ke baad.
  • Retired hurt: Injury ki wajah se batsman baad mein continue kar sakta hai (admin decision).
  • Obstructing the field: Batsman out declared ho sakta hai fielder ko rok ke.
  • Last wicket partnership allowed hai — no "last man standing" rule by default.
  • Toss loser ko bat ya bowl choose karne ka choice nahi hoga unless toss winner choose kare.

📊 POINTS SYSTEM

Points table mein team ki ranking Points → NRR → Wins ke order mein decide hogi. Net Run Rate (NRR) = (Total Runs Scored / Total Overs Faced) − (Total Runs Conceded / Total Overs Bowled)
  • Win: 2 points
  • Loss: 0 points
  • Tie/No Result: 1 point each (admin decision)
  • Abandoned match: Toss hone ke baad abandoned → 1 point each

👤 PLAYER ELIGIBILITY

  • Ek player sirf ek hi team ke liye khel sakta hai poore season mein.
  • Captain team ka representative hota hai. Match mein captain hi toss ke liye jayega.
  • Player transfer season ke beech allowed hai only with admin approval.
  • Minimum age: 10 years. Maximum: 60 years.
  • Players ko admin panel mein registered hona zaroori hai match se pehle.

⚖️ CODE OF CONDUCT

  • Respect umpire's decision — arguing will result in penalty runs or dismissal.
  • No aggressive behavior toward players, officials, or spectators.
  • Spirit of cricket — calling back batsmen, walking when out, informing about boundaries.
  • Match fixing ya tanking — team banned ho sakti hai tournament se.
  • Disputes admin panel mein report karo — umpire ka decision final hoga on-field.

🌟 FAIR PLAY AWARD

  • Har match ke baad dono captains Fair Play rating denge opponent team ko (1-5 stars).
  • Season end par highest average Fair Play score wali team ko Fair Play Award milega.
  • Fair Play Award winner ko bonus 1 point milega points table mein.
